Protoathon (B-Thrive)

In the Protoathon competition, I designed an app tailored for unique students at BINUS University who face challenges related to mental health and anxiety, making it hard to concentrate in crowded classroom settings. The app offers a supportive environment by enabling communication between students with similar experiences via chat. Additionally, it provides access to psychologists within BINUS, displaying their available schedules, and includes a daily short emotion quiz to monitor the users' well-being. The aim is to create a space where students can manage their mental health while continuing their studies effectively.

Key Features

Peer Support Chat

Enables students with similar mental health experiences to connect and communicate, providing a safe space for mutual support.

Psychologist Scheduling

Allows students to easily contact university psychologists and view their available schedules for appointments.

Daily Emotion Monitoring

Includes a short daily quiz to track students' emotional well-being, helping to monitor patterns and provide early intervention if needed.
