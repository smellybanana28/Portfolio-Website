Carbon Emission Calculator (Emi2C)

Emi2C is a web application designed to calculate carbon emissions generated by individual transportation activities. It provides users with a tool to assess their environmental impact and encourages sustainable transportation choices. The app aims to raise awareness about the consequences of carbon emissions, particularly in densely populated areas like Jakarta, Indonesia, where private transportation contributes significantly to traffic congestion and air pollution.

Key Features

Carbon Emission Calculation

Allows users to input details such as vehicle type, fuel type, and distance traveled to calculate their carbon dioxide emissions.

Educational Resources

Includes educational content on the consequences of carbon emissions and the importance of sustainable transportation practices.

User-Friendly Interface

Features an intuitive and easy to use interface for seamless navigation and input of transportation data.
