Mental Health Support Companion (MindMate)

MindMate is a prototype of a voice-based mobile application designed to provide mental health support to individuals who may feel isolated or unable to confide in others. The app serves as a virtual companion, allowing users to share their thoughts and feelings through voice input. The prototype demonstrates the core features of the app, including AI-powered conversation capabilities, emotion detection through voice analysis, and voice modulation to adapt the AI's voice based on user preferences or character selection.

Key Features

Voice Input

Allows users to express themselves by speaking directly to the AI companion, eliminating the need for text-based communication.

AI-Powered Conversation

Demonstrates the AI's ability to engage in natural language conversations, providing empathetic responses tailored to the user's emotions and needs.

Emotion Detection

Utilizes voice analysis algorithms to detect the user's emotional state based on their tone, pitch, and other vocal cues.

Technologies Used

Voice Recognition

Speech recognition APIs such as Google Speech-to-Text or Apple Speech Recognition for voice input.

Emotion Detection

Voice analysis algorithms or libraries (OpenSMILE) for detecting emotional cues in the user's voice.
